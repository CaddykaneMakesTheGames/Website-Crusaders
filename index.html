Website Crusaders

(A JoJo's Bizarre Adventure fansite)

Hello, and welcome to Website Crusaders, an unofficial website for the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure manga and animated adaptation where you can find all the latest news for JoJo's Bizarre Adventure and all its parts. You can find articles here or at the bottom part of the screen, you can submit an article of your own to be put on the site, and you can leave a word of encouragement in the comments section, among other things! You can also find the link to the (unofficial) JoJo wiki here, and some info about the series for beginners can be found here.You can also scroll down and check the bottom of the screen for our current features, as well as (soon) a changelog. So enjoy your stay! Communicate with others, learn more about your favorite characters and scenes, and maybe give us some tips while you're at it!


Right now there isn't much to look at. I haven't added any articles yet, but right now I'm trying to make a functioning and clean-looking website before I add articles. If you want an article to be submitted, submit it here.
